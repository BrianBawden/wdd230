Rule of Thirds

Amicalola Falls

Amicalola Falls

For my example of the Rule of thirds I decided to use this blog as my example because it talked about the rule itself. The part the I liked to most was the image of the man standing in front of the waterfall.

This image is a perfect example of the rule of thirds. Do you see how the man in the red jacket in on the right third and bottom third of the picture? By placing him here your eye is drawn to him, but quickly moves on to the beauty of the water fall too.

Alignment

YouTube

YouTube

I choose to use the YouTube website to show the rule of alignment. I picked it for two reasons. First, the category bar shows a row of words allowing the user to know that by clicking on one they can narrow their search by those categories. Secondly, just below the category's are smaller thumbnails for short videos, again their alignment shows that they are all short videos.
