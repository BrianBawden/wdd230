Rule of Thirds

Amicalola Falls

Amicalola Falls

For my example of the Rule of thirds I decided to use the Amicalola Falls website. The Falls are just 40 minutes from my house and I thought the picture showed good use of the rule of thirds by running the top of the water fall across the top third of the picture.

Alignment

YouTube

YouTube

I choose to use the YouTube website to show the rule of alignment. I picked it for two reasons. First, the category bar shows a row of words allowing the user to know that by clicking on one they can narrow their search by those categories. Secondly, just below the category's are smaller thumbnails for short videos, again their alignment shows that they are all short videos.
